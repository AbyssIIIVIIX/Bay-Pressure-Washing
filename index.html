Safety Practices

Safety is our top priority. We adhere to rigorous safety practices to ensure a secure and effective pressure washing experience for both our team and your property. Our trained technicians use personal protective equipment (PPE) such as gloves, goggles, and ear protection to safeguard against high-pressure water and cleaning agents.

  • Personal Protective Equipment (PPE): Ensure all technicians wear appropriate PPE, including gloves, goggles, and masks, to protect against chemicals, grease, and debris during the cleaning process.
  • Safety Precautions: Verify that the kitchen’s fire suppression system is operational and ensure the area is properly ventilated to prevent any risk of fire from flammable residues or cleaning agents.
  • Equipment and Electrical Safety: Disconnect all electrical appliances and equipment in the kitchen to avoid electrical hazards. Additionally, use only equipment specifically designed for hood cleaning to prevent accidents and ensure effective cleaning.
